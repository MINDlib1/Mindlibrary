The Founding of Mind Library

Mind Library is a digital library application officially launched on April 25, 2025. It was created with a clear mission: to make knowledge more accessible, inclusive, and integrated with modern technology.Born from the collaboration of educators, developers, and literacy advocates, Mind Library was designed to meet the growing demand for flexible, on-demand access to books and educational content. The app allows users to explore a wide range of digital books, journals, audiobooks, and learning resources — anytime, anywhere. More than just an e-book platform, Mind Library serves as a learning hub, offering features like personalized reading recommendations, study tools, community book clubs, and regular knowledge-sharing events. Guided by the vision of "Knowledge for All, Anytime, Anywhere," Mind Library is committed to bridging educational gaps and empowering learners around the world — one digital page at a time.

Visi

To become an innovative, inclusive and inspiring center for literacy and learning to create an intelligent, critical and globally competitive society.

Misi

  1. Providing Access to Quality Information

    Providing a collection of relevant, up-to-date, and easily accessible library materials to support learning, research, and self-development activities.

  2. Promoting a Culture of Literacy

    Inspiring interest in reading and fostering a culture of literacy in all groups through educational and participatory programs.

  3. Developing Library Service Innovation

    Integrating information technology to expand the reach and effectiveness of library services.

  4. Supporting Lifelong Learning

    To be an open space that encourages collaboration, creativity and continuous learning for all levels of society.

  5. Establishing Strategic Partnerships

    Collaborate with educational institutions, communities and other partners to strengthen the library's function as a center for knowledge and skills development.
